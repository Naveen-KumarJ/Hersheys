HERSHEY'S Double Strawberry Milkshake Recipe

Details

SKILL LEVEL:

Beginner

Yields:

2 glasses

Ingredients and Directions

Ingredients

1/2 cup

milk (cold)

2 to 3 scoops

strawberry ice cream

3 tbsp

HERSHEY'S Strawberry Flavored SYRUP

fresh strawberries

Whipped cream topping (optional)

Directions

Equipment Needed

1 BLENDER
2 MASON JARS

1

Place ice cream, milk and HERSHEY'S Strawberry Flavored SYRUP in a blender. Cover and blend until smooth.

2

Garnish with whipped topping and strawberries.
